About Me

Why Mindfulness?

HI! My name is Sandra and this is my first HTML class. Prior to this the only experience I had with any sort of coding was a bit of python. I admit taking a 9 week course semmed like a good idea at enrollment but as the class went on it did feel a bit difficult at times. With class, work and home life enjoying the little things can be difficult. Mindfulness has been something I have been trying to work on as of late in order to truly appreciate all the little things in the chaos.

Mindfulness is something that is talked about but in times of stress can go out the window. With finals approaching it's something that came to mind when thinking about a topic for the project. Mindfulness can act as a grounding tool when we are most busy and feeling overwhelmed. It felt like an appropriate topic for this project as I am trying to learn more about it, and working on the project had me focus on mindfulness as I created each page.
