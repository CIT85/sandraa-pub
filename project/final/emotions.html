Emotional Health

What is emotional health?

  • Emotional health is about how we feel on the inside and how we deal with our feelings. It's like having a toolkit for handling different emotions, whether they're good or bad. When we're emotionally healthy, we can manage stress, get along with others, and feel happy most of the time. It's all about knowing how to take care of our feelings, build strong relationships, and stay positive even when things get tough.

Why is it important to keep emotionally healthy?

  • Being emotionally healthy is important because it helps us feel good about ourselves and handle life's ups and downs better. When we're emotionally healthy, we can manage stress, solve problems, and build strong relationships with others. It's like having a superpower that lets us stay positive, resilient, and happy, no matter what challenges come our way.
