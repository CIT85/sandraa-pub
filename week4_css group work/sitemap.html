Color Palette

I used various suggested colors from Coolors suggestion palettes to make my own. In order to make sure the colors had good visibility and were easy to read I used Coolors contrast checker to check the colors against one another.

  • #1A090D Licorice
  • #5EF03F Walnut Brown
  • #BDB76B Dark Khaki
  • #DC93A2 Puce
  • #FFEFD5 Papaya Whip
  • #F2F4F3 Whitesmoke

Box Model

A representation of the box model on my page. The margin, border, padding and content have all been modified in order to make the content visually appealing and readable. 'Sourdough' now stands out with a color border that has been tailored to fit evenly around it by using padding and margins to make it look like a sign. I hope to apply this to more of my content in the future.
